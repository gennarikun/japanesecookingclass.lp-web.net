FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

01How many guests for a private booking?+

Private rooms from 8 guests. Large hall available for 30+ guests (up to 80).

02Are daytime banquets available?+

Yes, we open at 10:00. Daytime banquets welcome. Udon-only meals available without reservation.

03Is it family-friendly?+

Absolutely. Our restaurant is completely non-smoking, so children can enjoy a safe environment. Discounts for elementary school and under; infants are free.

04Is the all-you-can-drink self-service?+

Yes. Make drinks at your own pace and preferred strength. The zero-wait style is very popular.

05Is parking available? What about tour buses?+

We have a large parking lot in front. Tour buses welcome — ideal for group travel.

06Do you accept cashless payments?+

We accept credit cards (VISA, Mastercard, AMEX), PayPay, and cash.
